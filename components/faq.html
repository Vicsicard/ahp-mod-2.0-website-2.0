Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the AI Handshake Protocol and how it can benefit your website.

What is the AI Handshake Protocol?

The AI Handshake Protocol (AHP) is the bridge between your website and AI systems, ensuring proper recognition, attribution, and monetization.

Will AHP slow down my website?

No. AHP is designed to be lightweight (less than 10KB) and loads asynchronously, so it won't affect your website's performance or loading speed. It's compatible with any website platform or content management system.

How does AHP improve my website's AI visibility?

AHP creates a standardized communication channel between your website and AI systems. It provides structured metadata about your content, ensuring proper attribution, enhancing discoverability, and enabling AI systems to better understand and represent your content accurately.

Which AI systems currently support AHP?

AHP is currently supported by major AI systems including ChatGPT, Claude, Bard, and several other leading AI platforms. Our team continuously works with AI providers to expand compatibility and ensure your content is properly recognized across the AI ecosystem.

How does the monetization feature work?

AHP enables content creators to monetize AI usage of their content through our partnership network. When AI systems use your content for training or to generate responses, you can receive compensation based on usage metrics. The monetization dashboard allows you to track usage, set pricing tiers, and manage revenue streams from AI interactions.

Is there a free version of AHP?

Yes! The basic AI Handshake Protocol and weekly analytics reports are completely free to use. There's no credit card required and no subscription needed. We only charge for premium features when you decide they're worth implementing for your specific needs.
