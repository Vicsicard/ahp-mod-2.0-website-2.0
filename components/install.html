Quick Installation

Install the AI Handshake Protocol

Adding the AI Handshake Protocol to your website takes just a few minutes. No registration required to get started.

1
Free

Copy the Code

Copy the AHP script code from the box on the right. This lightweight code enables AI systems to properly recognize and index your website.

2

Add to Your Website

Paste the code into the <head> section of your website. If you're using a CMS like WordPress, you can add it through the theme settings or using a header/footer plugin.

3

Enter Your Email

After installation, a simple popup will appear on your site. Enter your email to receive weekly AI visibility reports and analysis. No account creation needed.

4

Get Your First Report

Within days, you'll receive your first AI visibility report with a detailed analysis of your site and actionable recommendations to improve your score.

AHP Installation Code
<!-- AI Handshake Protocol --> <script src="https://cdn.aihandshake.org/module.js"></script>
Note: The AHP script is lightweight (less than 10KB) and doesn't affect your website's performance. It only activates when AI systems visit your site.
